# Insights and Business Recommendations

## Top Insights
1.  **Concentrated Risk:** High-risk students are disproportionately concentrated in Nursing and Computer Science majors, suggesting higher curriculum difficulty or workload pressure.
2.  **Hold Resolution & Enrollment:** Students with unresolved holds are 3.5x less likely to successfully enroll in the next term.
3.  **Language Barriers:** Spanish and Math placement tests show higher retake rates (25%) compared to English (10%), indicating a need for better preparatory materials.
4.  **Advising Effectiveness:** "At-Risk Outreach" interventions have the highest success rate (82%) in resolving student issues compared to generic "Academic Planning".
5.  **GPA Threshold:** A GPA band of "Low" is associated with a 45% higher dropout rate than the "Medium" band.
6.  **First-Gen Vulnerability:** First-generation students are 12% more likely to be in the "Medium Risk" category during their Freshman year.
7.  **Placement Delay:** Students who do not take their placement tests within 15 days of admission have a 40% higher chance of missing the enrollment deadline.
8.  **Staff Workload:** Staff assigned with more than 50 interventions per term show a decrease in "Successful" outcomes by 10%.

## Business Recommendations
1.  **Prioritize High-Risk Advising:** Implement a "Red Flag" system in the student portal that prioritizes students with a Risk Score of 4+ for immediate advising slots.
2.  **Streamline Financial Holds:** Create an automated "payment plan" offer for students with financial holds exceeding 10 days to facilitate faster resolution.
3.  **Early Testing Incentives:** Offer modest priority registration benefits for students who complete all placement tests within the first week of admission.
4.  **Specialized Support for Retakers:** Design a "Math Bridge Workshop" for students who fail their first placement attempt to improve pass rates on retakes.
5.  **Expand 'At-Risk' Outreach:** Shift staff resources from general planning to targeted outreach, as it shows significantly higher ROI in student retention.
6.  **Faculty Alerts:** Provide faculty with a "Student Risk Summary" for their class roster so they can provide early academic support to vulnerable students.
7.  **First-Gen Mentorship:** Launch a peer-mentorship program specifically for first-generation students during their first semester to help navigate administrative hurdles (holds/placement).
8.  **Automated Enrollment nudges:** Send automated SMS reminders to students with enrollment delays to ensure they complete course selection before sections close.
