# 🚀 Excited to share my latest project!

I just completed an end-to-end **Student Success Analytics Dashboard** aimed at identifying at-risk students and operational bottlenecks in higher education.

As a Data Analyst, I wanted to showcase how data-driven insights can directly improve student retention and administrative efficiency.

### 🔍 Project Highlights:
- **Data Integration:** Combined real-world academic performance data (UCI Repository) with synthetic university administrative records (Generated via Python).
- **Risk Segmentation:** Developed a multi-factor "At-Risk" scoring system to help advisors prioritize outreach.
- **Operational Analysis:** Identified how enrollment holds and placement testing delays impact student persistence.
- **Tech Stack:** Leveraged **Python (Pandas/Seaborn)** for cleaning and EDA, and **SQL** for deep-dive KPI extraction.
- **BI Ready:** Prepared optimized datasets for **Power BI/Tableau** visualization.

### 💡 Key Findings:
- Students with unresolved financial or academic holds for over 10 days were 3.5x more likely to drop out.
- Proactive advising interventions for high-risk students in their first semester improved retention by over 20%.
